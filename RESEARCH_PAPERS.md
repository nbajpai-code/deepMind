# DeepMind Research Papers

A curated list of seminal papers from DeepMind.

## 1. Biology & Science

### AlphaFold
*   **AlphaFold 3 (2024):** "Accurate structure prediction of biomolecular interactions with AlphaFold 3" (Nature). [Link](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-024-07487-w)
*   **AlphaFold 2 (2021):** "Highly accurate protein structure prediction with AlphaFold" (Nature). [Link](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-021-03819-2)
*   **AlphaFold 1 (2018):** "Improved protein structure prediction using potentials from deep learning" (Nature).

## 2. Games & Reinforcement Learning

### AlphaGo / AlphaZero
*   **AlphaGo Zero (2017):** "Mastering the game of Go without human knowledge" (Nature). [Link](https://www.nature.com/articles/nature24270)
*   **AlphaGo (2016):** "Mastering the game of Go with deep neural networks and tree search" (Nature). [Link](https://www.nature.com/articles/nature16961)

### MuZero
*   **MuZero (2020):** "Mastering Atari, Go, chess and shogi by planning with a learned model" (Nature). [Link](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-020-03051-4)

## 3. General Intelligence & Multimodal Models

### Gemini
*   **Gemini 1.0 (2023):** "Gemini: A Family of Highly Capable Multimodal Models" (Google DeepMind Technical Report). [Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2312.11805)
*   **Gemini 1.5 Pro (2024):** "Gemini 1.5: Unlocking multimodal understanding across millions of tokens of context" (Google DeepMind Technical Report). [Link](https://storage.googleapis.com/deepmind-media/gemini/gemini_v1_5_report.pdf)

### Gato
*   **Gato (2022):** "A Generalist Agent" (DeepMind Research). [Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2205.06175)
